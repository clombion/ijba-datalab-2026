Based on the course materials for both years, the **2024-2025 curriculum** represents a strategic pivot from "executing the pipeline" to "designing the inquiry."

While the core **Data Pipeline** (Define, Find, Get, Verify, Clean, Analyze, Present) remains the backbone of both years, the 2024-2025 session introduces conceptual layers that force students to think more rigorously before they touch any software.

Here are the major evolutions:

### 1. Strategic Shift: From "Exploration" to "Design-First"
The most significant change is the introduction of a "Design Phase" that prevents students from rushing into data collection.

*   **2023-2024 (The "Search" Approach):**
    *   The curriculum moved quickly from defining a project to **Finding** and **Getting** data.
    *   The focus was on *locating* existing files (Open Data, APIs) and then figuring out what was inside.
*   **2024-2025 (The "Horizon" Approach):**
    *   **New Concept: The Horizon Table (*Tableau d'horizon*).** Before searching for data, students must now design the empty dataset they *want* to find, specifying exactly which rows (observations) and columns (variables) are needed to answer their hypothesis.
    *   **Impact:** This prevents "data drift" (getting lost in a dataset) by forcing students to define their destination before they start driving.

### 2. Pedagogical Shift: "Tabular Thinking"
The 2025 session introduces a new literacy module in Session 1 to train the "data eye."

*   **2023-2024:** Started with definitions of Open Data and the history of the field.
*   **2024-2025:** Introduces **"Tabular Thinking" (*La pensée tabulaire*)**.
    *   **Exercise:** Students are given complex published visualizations (e.g., car accidents by engine power) and must mentally "reverse-engineer" them back into a spreadsheet format.
    *   **Goal:** This trains students to understand data structure abstractly before they ever open Excel or Google Sheets.

### 3. Data Literacy: "Hallucinations" & Representation
The definition of "Data" has evolved to reflect the AI era.

*   **2023-2024:** Focused on technical verification—checking reliability, exhaustiveness, and quality using statistics and expert interviews.
*   **2024-2025:** Reframes data ontologically.
    *   Data is explicitly defined as a **"Structured representation of the world"**.
    *   **New Risk:** The curriculum now lists **"Hallucination"** alongside "Error" and "Falsification" as a core data risk. This suggests a modernization of the curriculum to account for AI-generated or synthetic data risks.

### 4. Case Study Evolution
The practical exercises have been updated to reflect more complex, sociological themes rather than purely political ones.

*   **2023-2024:** Focused heavily on the **2022 Legislative Elections**. The exercise was to calculate gender parity among candidates.
*   **2024-2025:**
    *   **"Rurbanization" & Digital Divide:** A new, complex case study breaking down the impact of urbanization on digital inclusion centers in Bordeaux.
    *   **Maternity Wards:** A case study on medicalization rates (C-sections/Episiotomies), introducing complex verification issues like "non-standardized encoding" of medical acts.

### 5. The Role of AI
*   **2023-2024:** AI (ChatGPT) was introduced at the very end as a "Helper" to write formulas or scripts.
*   **2024-2025:** AI is integrated more deeply.
    *   It is treated as a subject of scrutiny (checking for hallucinations).
    *   A dedicated section **"Les IA et le journalisme de données"** is now explicitly part of the analysis phase, suggesting AI is moving from a "tech tip" to a core methodological component.

### Summary Comparison

| Feature | 2023-2024 Session | 2024-2025 Session |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Starting Point** | Finding Sources & Open Data Definitions | **"Tabular Thinking"** (Reverse Engineering Charts) |
| **Design Methodology** | Define Project -> Find Data | **Horizon Table** (Design the empty table first) |
| **Data Definition** | Facts to be verified | **"Structured Representation"** susceptible to Hallucination |
| **Key Case Study** | 2022 Elections (Political) | **Digital Fracture & Maternities** (Sociological/Health) |
| **AI Role** | A tool for writing formulas (Assistant) | A subject of scrutiny and analysis (Component) |
