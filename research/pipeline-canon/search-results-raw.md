## NOTES ON KEY COLLECTIONS & META-RESOURCES

- **Dan Nguyen's GitHub syllabi collection** (item 100) contains links to dozens of university syllabi with their reading lists — a rich secondary source for identifying more articles and books.
- **GIJN Resource Center** (item 92) has 2000+ items across 14 languages; the data journalism topic page and methodology topic page are complementary.
- **IRE/NICAR tipsheets** (item 97) are the largest single repository of practitioner methodology, though access requires IRE membership.
- **Coda.Br** (item 90) is the main Portuguese-language data journalism conference, run by Escola de Dados.
- **datajconf.com** (item 87) proceedings are the most focused academic venue for computational journalism research.
- **Computation+Journalism** (item 88) papers are non-archival, meaning they may later appear in journals under different titles.
