_**Data as material**_

decisions in a broad spectrum of domains ranging from politics t
medicine. The material side of data also means data can be sold an

“a tool for communication”, data facilitates “intellectual and mate

which may be collected from “a non-reproducible situation” and su
fer errors and missing values. Patients’ discomfort may be involve
in data collection. Medical data analysis and interpretation requir
domain knowledge and consider patient characteristics.
Being material, data often exists collectively and grows in separat
silos, which means it is usually created separately to serve certain pu

may exist in different formats such as unstructured, structured an
